3.1: Philippine Photography and Film Making

Philippine Photography
Photography today is made easier through the invention of highly technical devices.
The introduction of digital cameras made photography at hand when needed.
Photography is an important part of almost every art career.
Without photographs, the works of artists in different fields would be words alone.
With prints, the world and work of artists come to reality.
Photography and Film Related Terms
  • Camera
    It is a lightproof object with a lens that captures incoming light and directs the light and resulting image toward film (optical camera) or the imaging device (digital camera).
  • Film
    In photography, it is a thin flexible strip of plastic or other material coated with light-sensitive emulsion for exposure in a camera used to produce photographs or motion pictures.
  • Still Photographers
    They consider the elements of art and principles of design as they capture moments in the film.
  • Sinibaldo de Mas y Sanz
    He was an adventurer and a poet who introduced photography in the Philippines in 1841.
  • Louis Daguerre
    He was a French artist who invented the early photographic process.
  • Eduardo Masferre
    Also known as the “Father of Philippine Photography,” Eduardo Masferre was a Filipino-Catalan photographer who made important documentary reports about the lifestyle of native people in the region of Cordillera in the middle of the twentieth century.
  • Focal Point
    It is the most important part of something, which you concentrate on or pay attention to.
  • Emphasis
    It is a principle of art that occurs any time an element of a piece is given dominance by the artist.
  • FPPF
    It stands for “Federation of Philippine Photographers Foundation”.
Remarkable Contemporary Photographers
  • George Tapan
    • Won two Pacific Asia Tourism Association (PATA) Gold awards.
    • Won an ASEAN Tourism Association Award.
    • First place in the 2011 National Geographic Photo Contest.
    • He has worked for the Department of Tourism and Philippine Airlines and has published five travel photography books.
  • John K. Chua
    • A remarkable seasoned advertising and commercial photographer with more than 40 years of experience in the Philippine photography industry.
    • John and his wife Harvey are the founders of Adphoto in the year 1978.
    • Loves to showcase the beauty of the Philippines and has won various local and international awards.
    • He is a pilot by profession, giving him an advantage in taking his aerial photography.
