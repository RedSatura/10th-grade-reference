2.1: Transcoding Linear and Nonlinear Text

Transcoding Linear and Nonlinear Text
To transcode means to convert something from one thing to another.
In order to transcode linear to nonlinear text and vice versa, we should be able to fully understand what the text is about.
To transcode, we must do the following:
  • Read and understand the source text to get its main idea
  • Extract important details from the text
  • Organize the information into categories
